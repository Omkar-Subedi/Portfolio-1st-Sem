A bar graph is a type of chart that uses bars to represent different values or categories. In the context of literacy rate of students, a bar graph could be used to show the percentage of students in a certain area or demographic who are able to read and write. Each bar in the graph would represent a different group of students (e.g. by age, gender, or socioeconomic status) and the height of the bar would indicate the literacy rate for that group. Bar graphs are useful for comparing the literacy rates of different groups and for visualizing changes in literacy over time.
